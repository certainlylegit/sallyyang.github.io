I'm

Final-year BSc Econometrics and Mathematical Economics student at the LSE

I’m Sally Yang, a final-year economics undergraduate.

After a brief encounter with competition economics as a clueless teen, I embarked on a bewildering journey of fancy theorems (IO, behavioural, macro, real analysis) and statistical voodoo (econometrics and machine learning).

Due to a spontaneous decision to write a quantitative thesis in what was supposed to be the most relaxing year of undergraduate life, I’ve since spent the last few months Temple Running from endogeneity.

My favourite R/Python palette is viridis and Stata graph scheme s1mono. In my spare time, I explore the cultural scene of London and dabble in graphic design. Recently, I’ve fallen in love with the manga Blue Period.
