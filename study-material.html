STUDY MATERIAL

  • by:Sally Yang
  • date: 1 July 2022

Here are my revision notes for LSE courses (2021/2022 academic year) EC313 Industrial Economics, EC333 Problems of Applied Econometrics and EC310 Behavioural Economics.

The headings may look a bit weird due to export settings from GoodNotes but remain readable. Lengthy proofs are sometimes minimised and placed next to the theorems. Zoom in to see them. The notes are incomplete owing to omittances on my part, and curriculum changes may render parts obsolete. All errors are probably my own.

I also include notes for legacy courses EC202 Microeconomic Principles II (MT LT), EC210 Macroeconomic Principles (MT LT) and EC221 Principles of Econometrics (MT LT) for good measure.
