ENDOGENOUS BEAVERS

  • by:Sally Yang
  • date: 18 May 2022

The Endogenous Beavers is an economic research-themed parody card game designed by Grace Choo and me.

In the summer of 2020. Grace and I became executive committee members of the LSESU Economics Society. We were brainstorming a new speaker souvenir for the Society's Amartya Sen Club. The previous souvenir was a generic white mug printed with the Society's logo. We wanted something more distinctive and memorable.

Inspiration came from a rather unusual place: A Kpop group called Oh My Girl released a new album, Nonstop. The album contained a monopoly game in the title track's visual style, and with its members as pieces.
